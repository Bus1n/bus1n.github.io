Learning Goals

Combining both Machine Learning with a practical approach to the Beauty Industry, this course allows participants gain insight into how we map out products and cater to specific users. By the end of this course, each student develops a simple cosmetics recommendation system.

A peak at your classes:

Class 1

What is Machine Learning (ML)?

The Future is Exciting! Ready?

Explore how ML works in the fields of Image Recognition, Product Recommendations, Social Media, Fake News, and Features. After gaining an overview of ML, you will specifically focus on developing a simple Product Recommendation algorithm by using Cosmetics datasets based on: ingredients, rank, price, and effects on the skin.

Class 2

Python Basics

Hello, World!

Practice giving computers instructions with your newly developed programming language -Python on a Google Colab! Google Colab is an easy-to-use online notebook that creates and shares documents containing live code.

Class 3

Data Analysis with Python

Harvest Insights From Your Beauty Data

Analyze prices, ranks, skin types, and the chemical components of more than 100 brands available on the market, including: CLINIQUE, Sephora Collection, GLAMGLOW, BIODERMA and much more. By the end, we will be comparing ingredients and deciding why some products outperform others.

Class 4

Using Machine Learning

Build a Simple Cosmetic Recommendation

In the final step, you will map out cosmetic items based on their ingredients similarities and give content-based filtering recommendations. Your contributions can help shoppers around the world every day!
