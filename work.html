For Work

With the easiness of deploy, manage and scale Wunity can bring together teams anywhere and offer high-quality customer service. Wunity is also available as on-premise for companies, and can be used via PC and mobile devices with its web browser based interface without any need of additional application.

Improve dynamic collaboration

Stay securely connected and well organized with us. Wunity accelerates and makes interdepartmental communication stronger while saving companies with a national or international distributed structure due to their structure or needs from travel expenses.

Easy, powerful management

Wunity has a powerful management module and easily integrates with your LDAP servers if you have. With its advanced reporting feature, you can also instantly monitor the resource consumption of the Wunity system running on your own servers.

Useful features

    Via the screen sharing feature,

  • share your presentations, videos,
  • graphs,
  • custom applications.

Wunity provides you transcript and the messages of the meeting as well.

Also it is possible to share one question survey with participants and have the result instantly even with a pie chart. By scheduling and add to calendar feature no meeting will be missed.
