Working Remote is popular again

Working remotely has quickly become a critical requirement for organizations around the world, and distributed teams are the new normal. According to a recent survey, remote workers said they were 29% happier at their jobs than field workers.

The participants who stated that they had a better work-life balance stated that they tended to work more hours during the working week and they also enjoyed doing this.

When managing a remote team, managers need to schedule a series of meetings to simulate face-to-face interactions such as one-on-one checks or team meetings. It is also of great importance to plan these meetings during normal working hours for all participants and to direct them with an agenda that offers everyone the chance to participate.

When managers of remote workers create a communication framework that supports sensitivity, transparency and integrity, employees perform at impressively high levels.

At this point, Wunity is at your side as an assistant that offers you all the features you need. Thanks to its user-friendly interfaces, the use of the system is pleasant for all kinds of participants.
