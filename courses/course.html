Enroll

Class 1: Storytelling – Discover, Build and Present Your Story

In this tutorial, girls are introduced to the art of storytelling through visuals and get to create their own personal story. It is an icebreaker to allow girls to discover what their story is and launch it by creating a personal storyboard.

Class 2: Photo Editing – Learn How Pictures Are Manipulated On Social Media

This tutorial explains to girls the concepts of photo-editing and manipulation. Girls begin to familiarise themselves with photo-editing tools and understand how the images they see on social media are altered. From here, they become architects of their own imagined visuals.

Class 3: WordPress – Reach A Global Audience Through Your Own Website

Girls are shown how to use and navigate around the most popular website creation software WordPress. Each girl creates her own website to present her story to the global audience.

Class 4: HTML Programming – Understand The Logic of Coding, A Skill That You Consume Everyday

During this tutorial, girls experiment with HTML programming language on their website. It allows them to see how coding is not a distant skill, but rather a purposeful tool to advance functions and designs on their website.
