About Us

Marija Musja

Founder & CEO

Donnie Connect provides online virtual learning courses for girls aged 11-18.

By definition, we are here to expand minds, offer critical thinking, and help young girls develop necessary digital skills and steppingstones towards a successful career.

My experience in different industries, such as human rights advocacy, company building, and project management led me to realize that deciding on a career in your most formative years is more stressful than it is hopeful. By connecting young girls with career professionals and industry experts, I want this online academy to open doors and provide limitless possibilities and a future full of thriving women.

Donnie Connect is a data science-backed intuitive matching program that correlate students’ unique personality type and strengths to specific digital courses taught by an industry professional.

For over four years, I ran a nonprofit association to organize virtual online courses for girls in the field of digital education. This allowed me to observe each girl’s unique character, motivation, and learning needs.

I believe education should serve as a tool to help young girls explore their abilities and talents. We should be raising girls’ self-esteem in the field of tech by expanding their understanding of different career opportunities that are available in the world of digital technologies and letting them play and experiment with different tools and subjects to find a career that truly resonates with each girl’s individual curiosity.

Our library and online curriculum courses for girls help both parents and teens feel more confident, secure, and excited about learning.
