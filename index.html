Our mission

HiRoster is a new recruiting approach, thatchanges the whole hockey transfer system.

Players fill out their profiles and send requests to teams and clubs. Teams submit requests for the next season with HiRoster.

Both sides will filter amd find each others offers, making it possible to get into an ideal team or sign a player for a season in a few clicks.

"I played for an unknown team and failed with the absence of a motivating salary or career opportunities..." - forget about this bad stories.

The HiRoster app makes it possible to determine what players there are available to the selected parameters and find a team which is seeking for a specified position player.

Hockey trafers have never been so simple and succesful!
