For Education

Wunity includes many features which are necessary for online communication in these days, such as video conferencing supporting high volume of participants, webinar, messaging, screen share and whiteboard. Wunity allows students by using voice and video to meet with their class and teacher.

Engage students online

Wunity syncs students even if they are not in the same physical environment. For large classes by virtual hand raising, white board and online survey features get the students more involved and interacted. Furthermore Wunity provides record and share the classroom sessions.

Easy content sharing

By Wunity easily share your web browser, entire monitor or just an individual app when you need. Furthermore participant pinning enables you to optimize the classroom layout for optimal discussion and feedback. It is also possible to interact with the student and lecturer one to one by messaging feature.

Useful features

With the scheduling feature all courses are on your calendar by one click. User friendly interfaces let all lecturers and students to use Wunity quickly. By advanced report it is easy to follow the attendance and interactions for each student at the courses.
