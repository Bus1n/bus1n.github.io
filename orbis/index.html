Architectural panels

Metl-Span Architectural panels offer thicker gauge facings to provide a more high-profile, monolithic appearance than standard gauges typically used for commercial and industrial projects. With trimless ends and pre-formed corners, our architectural panels eliminate the need for metal flashings allowing architects more design freedom.

Our story

Orbis Group of Companies began its operations in March of 2007 with humble beginnings. The goal was to build a solid and respectable company founded on seven principal core values: quality, performance, honesty, integrity, safety, commitment and dependability. We started out as small window wall installation crew, working in Vancouver and Calgary. Orbis promptly developed a reputation that aligned with the core values set by the ownership, and the natural progression to a successful business began to unfold. Orbis began to attract respected professionals within the building envelope industry with the vision of the ownership evolving the company into the multi-faceted corporation of today. The divisions within the company concentrate on placing the customer’s needs first. We focus on relationships, value engineering and solutions so our customers can meet the needs of today’s increasingly complex projects. We meet a wide range of demands from window wall, unitized curtainwall, custom glazing systems, architectural panel systems, and a multitude of other cladding & soffit systems for commercial, institutional and multi-family projects. We pride ourselves in our ability to operate as independent businesses under the same parent company, yet innovative and collaborative, complimenting each project team. When clients choose to work with the Orbis Group of Companies, they know they are working with a company true to its roots and the core values. Choose the Orbis team and rest assured your project will be completed to the highest standards.
