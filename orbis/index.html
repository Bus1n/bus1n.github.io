Our story

Orbis Group, founded in 2007 specializing in glazing installation. Over the years, Orbis has expanded into the building envelope sector enabling us to offer a full building envelope solution. Focusing in Calgary, expansion commenced throughout many major cities from Vancouver to Toronto while maintaining our primary focus in Western Canada. Orbis Group has maintained many great relationships by ensuring exceptional completion of each and every project throughout the years. As safety and efficiency evolved in the industry over the last several years, Orbis Group ensured that the highest standards were met consistently by following the province of BC stringent codes and implementing them across Western Canada. For full or partial building envelope needs, Orbis Group can help manage your project from conception to completion while ensuring that our clients needs are top priority.
