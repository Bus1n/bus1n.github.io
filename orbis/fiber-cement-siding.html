Fiber cement siding

Fiber cement has been used in the construction industry for almost a century, however, the first versions were not very successful as they were found to contain harmful materials such as asbestos. In the 1980’s, fiber cement siding products gained momentum when safer compositions were developed while still maintaining durable material properties. Today, fiber cement siding manufacturers use advanced production methods resulting in extremely strong an aesthetically pleasing panels designed to last decades in adverse weather conditions. Thanks to the undoubtable advantages of using fiber cement to clad your home or business, manufacturers have reached the forefront of the cladding industry across North America. Orbis Group works with the highest quality of fiber cement siding, such as James Hardie, Ceraclad, Acrytec and more to provide the best quality of siding cladding products backed by long warranty coverage to ensure peace of mind for our clients.

Ceraclad

Manufactured in Japan for more than 40 years, the Ceraclad panels are available in a wide range of patterns, textures and colors. The triple-coated finish provides 365 days self-cleaning, air prufying and comes with 20-year fade limited warranty.

Acrytec

The Acry NC panel is a glass fiber reinforced concrete panel. Colour, texture and design are customizable to help your vision for the project come to life. From the complete cladding of industrial/commercial projects to small residential accent areas, the possibilities are endless.
With the ability to offer pre-finished corner details, as well as modular sized panels that may be cleanly cut on site using standard power tools, Acrytec Panel Industries can provide a product that is brilliant, durable and long lasting, to meet your individual needs.
