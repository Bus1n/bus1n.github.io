Glazing

Glazing is critical in construction since it shapes the skyline of the city. In today’s world of concrete jungles, glass is what makes buildings unique. Orbis stays ahead of the competition with it’s dedicated design team, procurement department and in-house installation crew to satisfy the needs of present day design trends.

Window Wall

Window wall is where our bread and butter began. We have an extensive experience in window wall design, supply and installation for over a decade. Literally hundreds of thousand of linear feet of waterproofing and millions of square feet of actual glazing systems were installed by our people. Every single project we started was successfully completed.

The process always starts at the design stage. Open communication with a team of architects, developers and window manufacturers allows us to eliminate mistakes during installation. This process, paired with an extensive Quality Control program, leads to a flawless finish with complete sign-off and service work. With over 50 projects under our belt using window wall system, we are able to deliver the utmost quality, safety and reliability to our partners and clients.
