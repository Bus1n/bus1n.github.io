Glazing Cladding

Cladding

Orbis provides supply and install of siding and soffit products on commercial and and multi-family projects.
We understand the importance of controlling the budget and maintaining a schedule which is why each project has a dedicated team to maintain cost and schedule.
No matter the size of your project, our team looks for value-engineering and schedule accelaration opportunities.
Our top priority as your siding contractor is not only to ensure that the final product fully meets your expectations but that the entire process be seamless and stress-free for you.

Types of Projects

Highrise Buildings

Orbis has grown its reputation on the commercial-grade installation of glazing and cladding systems on high-rise projects. We take pride in our ability to service our clients from concept to reality. Our dedicated pre-construction team works with our in-house engineer to develop efficient details while exploring value engineering options to find ways to accelarate the construction schedule. We have developed our QAQC program in a way where we ensure once we leave an area, it’s been actually completed without the need to return for deficiencies.
This practice allows us to stay ahead of schedule and stand by our workmanship warranty.

Multi-Family

Most multi-family developments typically require an accelarated schedule in order to control costs and meet deadlines. We understand the inconsistancy of using subcontractors when it comes to scheduling and quality control - which is why Orbis employes a dedicated labor force of over 70+ workers. Being able to ensure our labor is properly trained in quality and safety, we are able to stay ahead of schedules and deficiencies. With over 20 years of multi-family experience, you can trust Orbis to deliver.

Commercial & Institutional

Having worked on projects such as schools and hospitals, we understand the need for functional material and competitive pricing. Orbis works with our partners on designing and fabricating efficienet cladding systems for commercial & insitutional projects. With our in-house installation team, Orbis stays ahead of schedule by ensuring a constant site presence with dedicated site supervisors, project managers and safety officers.
