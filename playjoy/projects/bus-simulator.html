Bus Simulator City Ride

PlayJoy Studios provided porting of this immersive 3D simulation game. We succeeded at bringing this amazing game both with stable performance for low-end devices and preserving beautiful visuals for high-end devices.

features:
  • Game performance optimization
  • Porting to Amazon Mobile and Amazon Kids+
  • Performance optimization and bug fixing
  • Co-development and bugfixing for Android and IOS
  • Optimizing models and art content
  • CPU / GPU optimization
  • SDK integration
  • Compliance testing
  • App Submission
description:

Take the driver’s seat to transport passengers around a lively city in licensed buses from famous manufacturers. Drive routes to complete campaign missions and unlock more buses, districts, routes. Climb the career ladder and build a public transport network.
